\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Manages a collection of various transportation modes within a city. 

The \mbox{\hyperlink{classTransportManager}{Transport\+Manager}} class provides functionalities for adding, managing, and accessing different transport modes such as airports, buses, ferries, and trains.
