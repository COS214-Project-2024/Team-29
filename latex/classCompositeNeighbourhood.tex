\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Represents a composite neighbourhood containing multiple building components. 

The \mbox{\hyperlink{classCompositeNeighbourhood}{Composite\+Neighbourhood}} class allows managing a collection of \mbox{\hyperlink{classBuildingComponent}{Building\+Component}} objects, enabling operations such as adding, demolishing, and calculating the tax income of buildings within the neighbourhood.
