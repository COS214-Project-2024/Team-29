\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Manages city operations, including budget, population, utility demands, transport, and government policies. 

The \mbox{\hyperlink{classCity}{City}} class provides functionality for managing various aspects of a city, such as buildings, utilities, transport, and policies, and calculates demands and satisfaction levels.
