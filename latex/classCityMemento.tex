\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Stores snapshots of city components, allowing the city state to be saved and restored. 

The \mbox{\hyperlink{classCityMemento}{City\+Memento}} class provides mechanisms to set and retrieve saved states of various city components, including satisfaction state, buildings, and modes of transport.
