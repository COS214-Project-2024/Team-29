\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Represents a policy with a name and an impact on citizen satisfaction. 

The \mbox{\hyperlink{classPolicy}{Policy}} class defines attributes for each policy, including its name and whether it has a positive or negative impact on the satisfaction of citizens.
